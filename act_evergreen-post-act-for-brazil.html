There are 400 million children living in poverty.
One person's Act of Compassion is enough to impact real change.

The Compassion community has raised $1,200,501 through 4,201 ACTS for Compassion to build better communities and release millions of children in Brazil from poverty.

Brazil is a country with a rich culture and strong faith, yet millions of Brazillian children live in extreme poverty and lack the resource to escape the cycle.

Despite the adversities, Compassion International is helping children in Brazil rewrite the story and rise above their cirumstances. These are stories of hope.

Compassion impacts 360° development: Mind, body, heart, and soul

The funds you raise will go to impact church-based child development centers in Brazil!

mind

  • After school tutoring
  • Assistance with school fees
  • Supplies and uniforms
  • Life-skiis and vocational training
  • Literacy classes, craft and music workshops
  • Infrastructure like classrooms and materials to run jobs and skills training workshops
  • Computer labs

body

  • Medical checkups, immunizations and care
  • Nutritional support
  • Education on health, sanitation and hygiene
  • Urgent medical care needs
  • Physical education spaces for motor skills development and safe places to place
  • Safe delivery and care for newborns and their moms

heart

  • Love and encouragement from personal sponsors
  • Loving attention from program staff
  • Development of respectful social skills
  • Christ-centered spiritual counseling

soul

  • Learning that God loves them
  • Singing and prayer
  • Age-and culture-appropriate Christian education
  • Service projects to help others
  • Spiritual development and socio-emotional support for children and their families
