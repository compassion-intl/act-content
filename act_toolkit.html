Three simple ways to get your fundraising rolling!

Make a donation yourself

Donating to your own fundraiser will demonstrate your commitment to the cause and encourage others to follow your lead.

Announce your fundraiser

Reach out to all the contacts in your social network and spread the word about your fundraiser.

Keep moving

Keep potential donors informed will increase the donations you'll receive. So don't be shy. Keep those updates coming!

Provide updates early and often.

Don't be shy about sending updates. A little nudge goes a long way.

Give your social network a final push.

Remember, most donations are typically made at the end of a fundraising campaign. So stay persistent! Give all your contacts one last chance to help out.

Your supporters deserve recognition.

After all, they're the ones who made your fundraising campaign a success. So make sure to acknowledge their generosity with an email, a tweet, or by sharing their kindness somewhere else. And, of course, inspire them to get involved with Compassion at the same time!
